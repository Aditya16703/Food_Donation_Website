# Food_Donation_Website

 • Authentication based on 3 roles- Donor, Food Bank, Organization
 • Donors can signup, login, donate food products to the desired food banks
 • Foodbank can signup,login and managethefood donations by donor and handles organization
 • Organization’s can login , signup and request food from food banks and manage food donation camps.
 • TechStack : HTML , CSS , JavaScript , ReactJs , NodeJs , MongoDB , ExpressJs.
